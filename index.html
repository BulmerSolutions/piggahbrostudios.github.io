Feline Fighters
About:

I have made this game with a different group of people than I do now. We were given a couple of months to create any game we want. I, Dylan, brought up the idea of creating a game inspired by "Duck Game" developed by Landon Podbielski.


Where to play:

This game is on my playcanvas page! All you need to do is click the link and click play!

How to play:

Controls:

  • Player 1:
    • Left: A
    • Right: D
    • Jump: W
    • Throw/Shoot: E
  • Player 2:
    • Left: J
    • Right: L
    • Jump: I
    • Throw/Shoot: O
  • Player 3:
    • Left: Left Arrow
    • Right: Right Arrow
    • Jump: Up Arrow
    • Throw/Shoot: Numpad 0

Piggah Bro's Technology Pack
About:

Piggah Bro's Technology modpack has been put together for Minecraft version 1.7.10 and is avalible on the Technic Platform website!


Installation:

To get this modpack, you'll need to download the Technic Launcher and login to your Minecraft account if you haven't done this already. From here, click the "Download This Modpack" on the sidebar, now at the top-left corner of that page will be a button saying install, click it and follow the intructions from there!
